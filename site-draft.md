## Hero

**I spent ten years selling other people's products.**

Marketing coordinator. Web3 content creator. Marketing lead at a startup called Hyperware. I got good at positioning, at storytelling, at finding the angle that makes someone care.

But I never built the thing itself. I just sold it.

**Then the job ended.**

Sixty days of runway. No severance, no backup plan. The standard move was apply for another marketing role, maybe start an agency, definitely panic.

I asked a different question: *What if I just build the products I used to sell?*

The problem was I don't code. Never learned. The idea of "learning to code" felt like a year of tutorials before I could ship anything.

**So I didn't.**

I found Claude Code. Then built an AI agent named Aiden. Now I describe what I want, he researches and executes, and I make the judgment calls on strategy and positioning.

Pure Gold — a trading journal — shipped in weeks, not months. PageData.dev — an API — generates revenue. JenPlan runs my life.

**The method is simple:** I strategize. Aiden executes. We ship.

This is how I'm figuring it out. Some projects will fail. Some will work. All of it gets documented here.

## The Method (Vibe Marketing)

**The workflow:**
1. Describe the product and positioning

3. Jen validates and refines
4. Ship in weeks, not quarters

**Why it works:** Marketing baked in from day one. Distribution considered before the first line of code.
